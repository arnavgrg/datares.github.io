What Is DataRes?

As of June 2018, 55.1% of the world's population has access to the internet. Each one of these people have a digital footprint that is a source of data – where one goes, what one eats, how much one spends - everything one does is grist to the data mill. Over the last decade, this has resulted in a massive explosion in both the data generated by organizations, as well as how organizations make data work for them by exploring insights and analytics into user and customer behavior.
